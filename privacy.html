Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Introduction

TT Content Publisher ("the App") is a personal content management tool. This privacy policy explains how data is handled by the App.

2. Data Collection

The App is for personal use by a single authorized user. No end-user data or third-party personal data is collected.

The only data processed by the App:

Data TypePurposeStorage
TikTok OAuth tokensAuthenticate with TikTok APILocal encrypted storage
Video content metadataTrack publishing scheduleLocal database
Publishing analyticsMonitor content performanceLocal database

3. Data Sharing

The App does not share any data with third parties. All data remains on the account owner's local device.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following services solely for content publishing:

  • TikTok API — to publish videos to the user's own account

These interactions are governed by TikTok's own privacy policy and terms.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on the user's device
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used
  • No data is transmitted to the App developer

6. User Rights

As the sole user of this personal application, the account owner has full control over all data and may delete it at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under 13. No data from minors is collected.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the repository owner via GitHub.
